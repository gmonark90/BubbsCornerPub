Rusty Rewards

Earn points on every visit and unlock perks like birthday drinks, exclusive tastings, and more.

How It Works

Sign up in person at the bar in under a minute. Start earning points right away with your first check.

  • 1 point for every $1 you spend on food and drinks (before tax & tip).
  • Every 75 points = $5 in bar credit.
  • Free birthday drink during your birthday week.
  • Early access to ticketed events and tastings.

Points and rewards have no cash value. See in-store signage for full terms and conditions.
